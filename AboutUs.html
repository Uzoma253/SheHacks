KIDS CAN CODE

This program was created in 2021 by Uzoma Uzoegwu, who is a Software Developper for Andela. It was the urgent need to train many developpers in Nigeria that birthed this idea in her mind.

This innovation is centered on kids because of their capacity to learn fast when they are young. It will help kids, between the ages of 3 and 15, explore their potentials in the fast growing world run by science and technology.

We seek to train kids to be great inventors who will contribute positively to the growth of the nation and the world at large.

This encourages them to think outside the box and beyond.
